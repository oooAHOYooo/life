Finding After-School Jobs in Connecticut

This page contains additional job opportunities and resources for finding after-school positions in AI, STEM, and general education roles throughout Connecticut. These positions are organized by type and location to help you find the best opportunities for your career goals.

Search Tips

  • Check organization websites directly for the most current job postings
  • Use job boards like Indeed and ZipRecruiter with specific search terms
  • Search for "after school," "STEM," "robotics," and "AI" combined with town names
  • Look for seasonal positions that align with school schedules
  • Consider volunteer opportunities as stepping stones to paid positions
