## Dandy Dandy Dandy

Dandy Dandy Dandy
was the name upon the game
i got it used in Branford
and i've never been the same
it had a box so dented
I mistook it for some trash
but after playing dandy dandy i learned
it's quite the bash

Dandy Dandy Dandy
was a hit among the fam
we played it after coffee and the stew and christmas ham
you start with just the two of spades
and roll a little die
you roll and roll and roll and roll - until the snake eyes cry

oh Dandy dandy dandy
is a game of quiet skill
you take a turn and kill a queen and
shout "Happy Daffadills"
Dandy Dandy Dandy
got its start in rural Spain

Dandy Dandy Dandy

some snake eyes or some boxcars you could start the bloody bout
move a knight into the rook

but inside, it was certain,

and in it was a board a square or two
you could play it solo dolo, but three or more to vandaluew
