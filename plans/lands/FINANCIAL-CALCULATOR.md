## MONTHLY PAYMENT CALCULATOR

Use this formula to calculate YOUR monthly payment:

```
Monthly Payment = (Loan Amount × Monthly Rate) / (1 - (1 + Monthly Rate)^-300)

Where:
- Loan Amount = Total Project Cost - $7,500 (your down payment)
- Monthly Rate = 0.09 / 12 = 0.0075
- 300 = number of months (25 years × 12 months)
```

**Example** (Goldilocks Scenario):
```
Loan Amount: $175,000 - $7,500 = $167,500
Monthly Rate: 0.0075
Monthly Payment = ($167,500 × 0.0075) / (1 - (1.0075)^-300)
               = $1,256.25 / 1.108
               = $1,134/month (principal & interest only)
```
