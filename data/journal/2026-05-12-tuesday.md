## Late Night / A Poem in Progress

**Time:** Late

---

*this is the story of how —*
*meow, got its meow*

*a little late —*
*a small bow*
*a tiny ship*
*a single brow*

*meow was a vagabond —*
*not knowing where to fro*
*so he went to the river*
*to talk to his reflection in the ripple*

*he noticed he had something in his teeth*
*so he went to the doctor*
